How to Install the SyncUp Add-on

Follow these steps to install the SyncUp add-on to your Google Sheets:

  1. Open Google Sheets and click on the “Add-ons” menu.
  2. Select “Get add-ons” to open the Google Workspace Marketplace.
  3. Search for “SyncUp” and select it from the search results.
  4. Click “Install” and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  5. Once installed, go back to Google Sheets, click on “Add-ons” > “SyncUp” > “Start” to launch the add-on.
